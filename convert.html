Coordinate Formats

FizzyCalc accepts coordinates in a variety of formats:
FormatExamples:
Decimal degrees47.629617,-122.105167
Degrees with decimal minutesN 47° 37.777 W 122° 06.310
Degrees, minutes and secondsN 47 37 46.62, W 122 06 18.60
N 47 37' 46.62", W 122 04' 18.60"
UTM10T E 567227 N 5275523
In general:
  • The degree symbol (°) is ignored.
  • If the cardinal direction is not indicated, a comma must separate the latitude and longitude.
  • If the cardinal direction is not indicated, a positive value will be interpreted for north and east, respectively.
